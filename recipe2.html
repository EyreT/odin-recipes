BAT Tortilla Chips with Mango Salsa

Ándale chiquitos!

Ingredients

For the Bat Tortilla Chips:

  • 6 (6-inch) flour tortillas
  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

    • For the Mango Salsa:

  • 1 mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 jalapeño pepper, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/3 cup chopped red onion
  • Juice of 1 lime

For the bat tortilla chips, you will need flour tortillas, nonstick cooking spray and then some type of seasoning. I like chili powder and salt, but a prepared seasoning like Tajin works great, too). Use small bat cookie cutters to cut bats from the tortillas. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray and arrange the bats in a single layer. Spray the bats with cooking spray and sprinkle with the chili powder and salt. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until you have crispy chips!

While the chips are baking, make a quick mango salsa for them by stirring together chopped mango,
jalapeño pepper, tomato, red onion and the juice of a lime.
